Game Instruction

  • 1. Player 1 can choose to compete with Player 2 or Bot.
  • 2. If the user chooses to compete with Player 2, Player 1 must input the Player 1’s name and Player 2’s name.
  • 3. The game board consists of a 10x8 hexagon block. 12. Disabled hexagon will appear in the random hexagon with grayish-color:
    a. 4 disabled hexagon for easy level
    b. 6 disabled hexagon for medium level
    c. 8 disabled hexagon for hard level
